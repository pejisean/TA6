Kinofilms

Kinofilms operates independent cinemas across the Philippines that specialize in screening Filipino independent and arthouse films. This provides an important platform for Filipino filmmakers to reach audiences beyond the mainstream commercial theaters.

The company aims to promote Filipino cinema and culture by offering a diverse selection of local films, from award-winning festival favorites to lesser-known gems. This includes both new releases as well as retrospective screenings of classic Filipino films.

In addition to film screenings, Kinofilms also hosts filmmaker Q&As, panel discussions, and other events to engage the local film community and educate audiences about the art of Filipino filmmaking.
